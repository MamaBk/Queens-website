About Me

I have a 7 year background in finance as I worked in standard bank as a prestige and private banking consultant. More About Me

Im an ADA graduate

ADA came at a perfect point in my life. I knew nothing about technology. Every little thing we did excited me because I knew it was leading me to a better place. My 1st website attempt was just words and pictures because we were being taught HTML5 - it was fulfilling to see what I could do, even with a language that is so simple. Thinking that was that, CSS was introduced. CSS is a way for you to style your content on the website. It blew my mind even more to see all the beautiful things i could do. Then Javascript came in to play - that was when I started seeing my sites coming to life even more,JavaScript is the Programming Language for the Web. It is where you can update and change both HTML and CSS. With JavaScript you can calculate, manipulate and validate data, basically manage the functionality of the site. . - it swept me off my feet. I am grateful to ADA for having introduced me to the world of technology. I now know it is what I want to do - before I just didn't know much about software development.

We did a lot of projects to enhance our skills and put into practice what we have learned. My personal favourites included building a website for apple watches and creating fun animations with the Super Mario Brothers on the browser - we even attempted to make games. I tried to recreate Tetris and that was mind blowing indeed. My most favorite is the newspaper project where we had to improve a newspaper site. In this project, we learned JavaScript in depth and other systems like Cmder and Github. There is still a whole lot more to learn but I am blessed to have been exposed to all of this in my time at ADA. I can't help but continue to be curious and see what else is out there for me!
