About Me

I am a fun loving person who enjoys spending time with family and friends. I a mother to a 2 year old boy and am always seeking a better version of myself in every shape or form. I have a 7 year background in finance as i worked in standard bank as a prestige and private banking consultant. I am very passionate about technology and anything that brings about a positive change

I love consistency and take criticism well even though its intended to put me down simply because I understand that anything good can be made better. I am very solution driven and will to help whenever needed. I am very generous and love sharing everything new learned

I'm an ADA graduate!

ADA came in my life as a point I'd consider perfect. I knew nothing about technology. Every little thing we did excited me because I knew it was leading me to a better place. My 1st website attempt was just words and pictures because we were being taught HTML5, It was fulfilling to see what I could do. Thinking that I was complete, CSS was introduced. It blew my mind even more to see all the beautiful things i could do, then Javascript came in to play, that was when I started seeing my sites coming to life even more and that swept me off my feet. I am grateful to ADA for having introduced me to the world of technology. I knew it is what I wanted to do, I just didn't know though that my passion lies in the path of software development.

We did a lot of projects to enhance our skills and put into practice what we have learned. My personal favourites are the apple watch website, the mario and luigi projects, we even attempted to make games. I tried tetris and that was mind blowing indeed. My most Favorite is the newspaper project where we had to improve a newspaper site. Here we learned JavaScript in depth and other systems like comder and Github and my team work was enhanced too, there is still a whole lot more to learn but i am blessed to have been exposed to all of this in my time with ADA, i can't help but continue being hungry to learn and see what else is out there for me.
